takes place

on Monday, September 27, 2058

Search in the Archive on keyword: Vari,
brief excerpts:

1 Vari is approximately 1000 earth-days.

A Vari can be divided into 25 spatia;
each spatium consists of 40 days.
When the last spatium has less than 40 days, it is called an epagomene.

This epagomene can be used to synchronize time across planets and spaceships. An epagomene can be shorter than forty days – never longer.

For more information on double time-accounting,
search keyword: Trans.
